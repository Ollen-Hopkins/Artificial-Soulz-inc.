What is Intelligence?

For Centuries, human intelligence down to its core is defined two ways.

  1. Logical thinking
  2. Empathy

Logical thinkers tend to analyze a situation or problem using reason and coming up with potential solutions. Logical thinkers gather all the information they can, assess the facts, and then systematically decide the best way to move forward.

Empathy is the ability to recognize, understand, and share the thoughts and feelings of another person, animal, or fictional character.
